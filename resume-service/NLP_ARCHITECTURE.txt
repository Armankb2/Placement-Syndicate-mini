2. THE CONTEXT SPECIALIST: SENTENCE TRANSFORMERS
-----------------------------------------------------------
How does a computer understand that "Lead Developer" and 
"Engineering Manager" are almost the same thing?

STEP A: EMBEDDING (THE MATH OF IDEAS)
Every sentence you write is turned into a "Vector". A Vector is 
just a long list of 384 numbers. These numbers represent the 
"DNA" of the idea.

STEP B: THE 384-DIMENSIONAL SPACE
Imagine a giant map. 
- "Apple" (the fruit) is at coordinates (2, 5).
- "Orange" is at (2.1, 5.1). They are very close on the map!
- "Computer" is at (50, 90). It's very far away.

Our model (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) has 384 different "dimensions" 
on its map! It maps every professional skill, role, and 
project into this massive coordinate system.

STEP C: COSINE SIMILARITY (MEASURING CLOSENESS)
When you upload a resume, the AI:
1. Turns your resume into a math coordinate (Vector).
2. Looks at all the interview experiences in MongoDB.
3. Calculates the "Distance" between your resume and those records.
4. The records with the smallest distance (highest similarity) 
   are your matches!
